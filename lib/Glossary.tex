\chapter{Glossary}\label{Glossary}

\glossaryterm{base bonus} A base bonus is the value of a bonus before any temporary modifiers or effects. For example, your base Fortitude defense bonus is equal to the Fortitude defense bonus granted by your class.

\glossaryterm{difficult terrain} Difficult terrain costs double the normal movement cost to move through.

\glossaryterm{hit value} Your hit value is the number of hit points you gain per level. Your hit value is equal to half your Constitution, half your Willpower, or half your base Fortitude bonus, whichever is higher.
