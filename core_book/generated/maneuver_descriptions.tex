\hypertarget{maneuver:Brace for Impact}{}
\begin{freeability}[Rank 1]{Brace for Impact}[\glossterm{Swift}]
\targets{Yourself}
You take half damage from \glossterm{physical} damage this round.
This halving is applied before \glossterm{resistances} and similar abilities.
Because this is a \glossterm{Swift} ability, it affects damage you take during the current phase.

\rankline
\rank{3} You also gain a \plus1 bonus to all defenses.
\rank{5} You also take half damage from \glossterm{energy} damage this round.
\rank{7} The defense bonus increases to \plus2.
\parhead{Lists} Esoteric, Martial, Primal, Wild
\end{freeability}
