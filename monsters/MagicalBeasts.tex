    \begin{monsection}{Aranea}{5}[2]
        \begin{spellcontent}
            \begin{spelltargetinginfo}
                \pari \mb{Senses} Low-light vision
                \pari \mb{Movement} 30 ft.\ land speed, 20 ft.\ climb speed
                \pari \mb{Size} Medium; \mb{Reach} 5 ft.
            \end{spelltargetinginfo}
            \begin{spelleffects}
                \pari \mb{HP} 82; \mb{Defenses} AD 21, Fort 17, Ref 19, Ment 22
                \pari \mb{Attacks} Bite \plus10 (1d8\plus3); \mb{Strikes} 2
                \pari \mb{Special Attacks} Poison \plus7 vs Fort (3d6, 7d8) [Immediate]
                \pari \mb{Alacrity} Extra action every other 
                \pari \mb{Attributes} Str 2, Dex 6, Con 0, Int 6, Per 4, Wil 8
            \end{spelleffects}
        \end{spellcontent}
        \begin{spellfooter}
            \pari \mb{Level} Magical beast 5 [Adept]
            \pari \mb{Encounter} Temperate forests; solitary or colony (3--6)
            \pari \mb{Abilities} Natural armor \plus4
        \end{spellfooter}
    \end{monsection}
    \parhead{Traits} Humanoid Form, Natural Venom, Spellcaster, Spit Web.


    \parhead{Humanoid Form} As a standard action, an aranea can spend an adept point to change into a humanoid or hybrid form.
    The appearance and race of its humanoid form depends on the aranea, but any individual aranea has a consistent appearance.
    While in humanoid form, an aranea cannot use its bite, poison, or web attacks, but it can cast spells.
    While in hybrid form, an aranea appears humanoid and can use all of its abilities, but a DR 20 Awareness check reveals its spider-like characteristics.

    \parhead{Poison} If an aranea damages a target with its bite attack, it can inject poison as an immediate action.
    The poison's potency is 7.
    The primary effect deals 3d6 poison damage.
    The terminal effect deals 7d8 poison damage.

    \parhead{Spells} An aranea can cast arcane spells by spending an adept point.
    It knows \spell{mage armor}, \spell{create image}, and \spell{sleep}.
    Its spellpower is 7.
    In a typical combat, an aranea has already cast \spell{mage armor} to gain an armor bonus before the fight starts.
    This bonus is included in its statistics above.

    \parhead{Web} As a standard action, an aranea can spit a web at a creature or object within \rngmed range.
    It makes an attack vs. Reflex against the target with a \plus7 accuracy.
    Success means the target is \immobilized for 2 rounds.
