            \begin{spelltargetinginfo}

                \pari \mb{Movement} 30 ft.\ land speed, 30 ft.\ fly speed

            \begin{spelleffects}
                \pari \mb{HP} 48; \mb{Defenses} AD 17, Fort 14, Ref 17, Ment 24
                \pari \mb{Attacks} Touch \plus10 (1d6\plus2); \mb{Strikes} 1

            \end{spelleffects}
        \end{spellcontent}

    \parhead{Traits} Draining Touch, Incorporeal, Magical Ability.

    \parhead{Babble} As a standard action, an allip can spend an adept point to babble incoherently.
    It makes a \plus5 vs. Mental attack against all creatures within an \areamed radius of it.
    Success against a target means it is \disoriented for 2 rounds.
    Critical success means it is \confused for 2 rounds instead.
    This is a \glossterm{Auditory}, \glossterm{Compulsion}, \glossterm{Mind} effect. 

    \parhead{Draining Touch} If an allip touches a creature, the touched creature takes 1d6 \add 2 life damage.
    The allip heals hit points equal to half the damage dealt in this way.
    In addition, it is proficient with its touch attack, gaining the normal \plus4 accuracy bonus for proficiency.

    \parhead{Incorporeal} Allips are \glossterm{incorporeal}.
